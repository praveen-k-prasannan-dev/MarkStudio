MarkStudio Editor — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

MarkStudio Editor is a local, offline Markdown document editor. This policy explains what data the application does — and does not — collect.

No personal data is collected

MarkStudio Editor does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal information. It contains no telemetry, no analytics, and no advertising.

What stays on your device

  • The Markdown documents you open, edit, and save remain wherever you choose to save them, under your control.
  • Application settings (theme, window size, view preferences) are stored locally at %APPDATA%\MarkdownEditor\settings.json.
  • Recovery drafts for unsaved changes are stored locally at %APPDATA%\MarkdownEditor\autosave\.
  • A diagnostic log used for troubleshooting errors is stored locally at %APPDATA%\MarkdownEditor\app.log. This log never leaves your device.

Network access

The live preview and PDF export features use the Microsoft Edge WebView2 component to render your own document locally on your device. MarkStudio Editor itself makes no network requests. If a document you author contains a link to a remote image or resource, the preview may load that resource directly from its source — the same way a web browser loads a page you open — because it is content you chose to reference, not something MarkStudio Editor collects or transmits on its own.

Third parties

MarkStudio Editor does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's privacy

MarkStudio Editor does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children, because it does not collect information at all.

Changes to this policy
